Tools for Carving

The following tools are helpful for carving!

Learn how to create them!

Handaxe

Needed Materials: Medium-sized rock (preferably Flint), smaller rock

*Take the smaller rock, and using it, tactfully strike the larger rock's edges.

*Go all the way around the large rock, until all the edges are exposed.

*Sharpen one of the edges of the large rock by shaving it down using the smaller rock.

(Make sure that the entire rock is not sharp, so that you are still able to use it without injuring yourself!)

Once you have sharpened one edge of the rock, you have created your handaxe! Use for any of your carving needs!

Chisel

Needed Materials: Large rock, small rock (preferably Flint), water, clay

*Place the large rock on a flat surface.

*Scrape the small rock against the large one, shaving down the small rock until all of the inner material is exposed.

Mix water and clay together.

Pour the mixture on top of the large rock.

Continue scraping the small rock against the large rock, now in the mixture.

(Make sure to only do the top part of the rock!)

Keep doing this until the top part of the rock is completely smooth and thin

Once you have done this, you have created your chisel! Use for all of your carving needs!
