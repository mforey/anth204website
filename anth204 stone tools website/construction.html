Tools for Construction

The following tools are helpful for constructing!

Learn how to create them!

Hammer

Needed Materials: Thick and rectangular rock, sharp and small rock (preferably flint), thick stick of wood, large rock

*Place the thick, rectangular rock on a flat surface.

*On the top of the rock, attempt to make a hole in the middle by shaving the middle down with the small and sharp rock.

*Once you have created a hole that goes through the rock, place the large rock on a flat surface.

*Shave the rock with the hole on the large rock, smoothing out the rough edges.

*Once you have done that, shave the top of the thick stick of wood so that it fits into the hole of the rock.

*Insert the stick into the rock's hole. Make sure it is secure!

*Once you have done that, you have created your hammer! Use it for any of your construction needs!

Saw

Needed Materials: Flake of flint, small rock

*Using the small rock, straighten and thin out one of the edges of the flake of flint, making it sharp.

*Once you have sharpened the side of the flint, make jagged edges on it (like a saw) using the small rock.

(Make sure the edges are subtle, so that it is not too hard to use.)

*Once you have done that, you have created your saw! Use it for all of your construction needs!
