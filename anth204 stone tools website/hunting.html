Tools for Hunting

The following tools are helpful for hunting!

Learn how to create them!

Spear

Needed Materials: Long rock (preferable flint), large rock, small rock, water, clay

*Using the smaller rock, shave off the edges of the long rock until all the edges are exposed.

*Place the large rock on a flat surface.

*Shave the long rock against the large rock, sharpening it as much as possible.

*Use the smaller rock to assist in shaving down if needed.

*Mix water and clay together.

*Pour the mixture on top of the large rock.

*Scrape and sharpen the long rock against the large rock, now in the mixture.

*Once the rock is smooth and sharp, you have created your spear! Use it for all of your hunting needs!

Arrowhead

Needed Materials: Small rock (preferably flint), large rock, water, clay

*Place the large rock on a flat surface.

*Shave the small rock against the large rock, until all of the small rock is exposed.

*Mix water and clay together.

*Pour the mixture on top of the large rock.

*Scrape and sharpen the top of the small rock against the large rock, now in the mixture.

*Make sure you created a sharp and thin point from the top of the rock. It should be able to pierce if thrown from a distance.

*Once you have made a sharp point, you have created your arrowhead! Use it for all of your hunting needs!

(If you would like, you can attach the arrowhead to the top of a sturdy stick to create an arrow!)
